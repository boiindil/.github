## Sponsorship

Sponsorships support independent open reference work and the preparation of small, reviewable artifacts.

GitHub Sponsors may require account-side approval and setup before the native sponsor route becomes active.

A sponsorship does not create:

- a consulting engagement
- legal, financial, medical, or procurement advice
- access to private IP
- operational support obligations
- a license to proprietary artifacts
- exclusivity or commercial usage rights

Commercial review, licensing, internal use, white-label, OEM, embedded, or exclusive rights are handled separately by written agreement.
